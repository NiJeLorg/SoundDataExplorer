Welcome to the Sound Health Explorer!

This site lets you review the health of any beach on Long Island Sound. The default view shows 5 years of beach testing data and you can change that timeframe to any scale you like.

There is other water quality-related information to explore including land use, location of sewage treatment plants, subwatershed boundaries, and more!

Click "Enter Site" to see the interactive map and learn more about the data behind the grades. Click "View Site Tutorial" for a slideshow explaining how to get the most out of the Sound Health Explorer.
